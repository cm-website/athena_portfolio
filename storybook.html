Featured Publication

"We Don't Deserve This"

Published by Page Publishing

A spellbinding tale of magic and mystery following a young girl on an action-packed journey of self-discovery. The story follows multiple characters in a world where humans have been eliminating supernatural creatures.

Main Characters:

  • Ammolite - A teenager who discovers she isn't human and becomes involved in a war to help her kind survive
  • Xavier - Part vampire, neglected by both humans and creatures, struggling with self-acceptance
  • Yuna - A werewolf soldier often dismissed as naive or too young despite her potential
  • Crimson - A human Hunter and daughter of the human leader with secret friendships among creatures
